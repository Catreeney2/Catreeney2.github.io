His Dark Materials

The His Dark Materials books by Philip Pullman are a series of 3 books, published in 1995, 1997 and 2000. It follows the coming of age of two children, Lyra Belacqua and Will Parry, as they wander through a series of parallel universes.

Author:

His Dark Materials was written by Philip Pullman

Books in the series:

  1. The Northern Lights
  2. The Subtle Knife
  3. The Amber Spyglass
